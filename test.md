# Common Abbreviations

Reference document for abbreviations used across the Miro Notes vault.

## Teams & Organizations
- **AMPED** - Apps, Marketplace, and Extensibility & Developer Experience (org/stream)
- **LA** - Layout Architecture (team)

## Projects
- **EntC** - [[Enterprise Customisation]]
- **IW** - Information Workspace (historical project name)
- **QA** - Quality Assurance (also see [[Project/QA]])

## Roles & People
- **EM** - Engineering Manager
- **PM** - Product Manager
- **TPM** - Technical Program Manager
- **IC** - Individual Contributor
- **TP** - Tech Partner

## Works Council
- **BR** - Betriebsrat → [[Berlin Works Council]]
- **WC** / **WoCo** - Works Council (could be [[Berlin Works Council]] or [[Amsterdam Works Council]])
- **WG** - Working Group (in works council context)
- **BV** / **WA** - Betriebsvereinbarung / Works Agreement
- **MBR** - Mitbestimmungsrecht / Co-determination Right

## German Law (Betriebsrat)
- **BetrVG** - Betriebsverfassungsgesetz (Works Constitution Act) → [[Betriebsrat/Laws/Law Code/BetrVG]]
- **BAG** - Bundesarbeitsgericht (Federal Labour Court)
- **LAG** - Landesarbeitsgericht (State Labour Court)
- **ArbG** - Arbeitsgericht (Labour Court)
- **ArbZG** - Arbeitszeitgesetz (Working Time Act)
- **KSchG** - Kündigungsschutzgesetz (Protection Against Dismissal Act)
- **BUrlG** - Bundesurlaubsgesetz (Federal Holiday Act)
- **AGG** - Allgemeines Gleichbehandlungsgesetz (General Equal Treatment Act)
- **GG** - Grundgesetz (Basic Law / Constitution)

## Technical Systems & Versioning
- **MSv1/v2** - ModeSystem v1/v2 → [[Project/ModeSystem]]
- **ASv1/v2** - AppSystem v1/v2 → [[Project/AppSystem]]
- **LPAPI** - Layout Panel API → [[Project/Layout Panel API]]
- **CIS** - Content Item Service (see [[Project/Brand Center]], [[Project/Data Tables]])
- **PAS** - Platform Application Service
- **PAO** - Platform Application Object

## Leadership & Org Structure
- **LT** - Leadership Team
- **XLT** - Extended Leadership Team
- **ELT** - Engineering Leadership Team
- **PLT** - Product Leadership Team

## Performance & HR
- **PIP** - Performance Improvement Plan
- **H1/H2** - Half 1 (Jan-Jun) / Half 2 (Jul-Dec) for performance review cycles
- **FY** - Fiscal Year
- **OKR** - Objectives and Key Results
